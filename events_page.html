Events

Upcoming Events
 Join us for an exciting calendar of events at Gracewell Methodist Church that cater to all ages and interests. Whether you're looking to deepen your faith, connect with fellow believers, or simply have a great time with your community, we have something for you. From inspiring worship services and thought-provoking Bible studies to family picnics and volunteer opportunities, our events are designed to foster fellowship, spiritual growth, and service. Explore our upcoming events below, mark your calendars, and come be a part of the vibrant life of our church!


Worship Services
 Our Sunday worship services are the heart of our church life. Join us each week for a time of uplifting praise, meaningful prayer, and inspiring sermons that draw us closer to God. We offer both traditional and contemporary services to cater to different worship preferences, and all are welcome to come and experience the presence of the Holy Spirit. Check out our schedule for service times and special worship events throughout the year, including Christmas and Easter services that celebrate the birth and resurrection of our Lord


Community and Outreach
 At Gracewell Methodist Church, we believe in making a positive impact in our community and beyond. Join us for various outreach and volunteer events where we extend Christ's love to those in need. From food drives and clothing donations to local service projects and mission trips, there are numerous ways to get involved and serve others. We also host community-building events like neighborhood clean-ups, picnics, and festivals that provide opportunities to build relationships and share the message of God's love with our neighbors.


Special Events and Celebrations
 Throughout the year, we come together to celebrate important milestones and seasons in the life of our church. From anniversaries and baptisms to weddings and baby dedications, these special events remind us of the blessings of community and faith. We also host seasonal gatherings such as Easter egg hunts, fall festivals, and Christmas pageants that create cherished memories for our families. Stay tuned to our event calendar for updates on these joyful occasions and how you can be a part of them.

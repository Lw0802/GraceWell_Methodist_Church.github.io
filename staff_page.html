Staff

Pastor John Smith
Pastor John Smith has been leading our congregation for over two decades with unwavering dedication and a deep passion for spiritual growth. He holds a Master of Divinity degree and has a heart for spreading the message of love, hope, and faith. Pastor John is not just a spiritual leader; he's also a compassionate counselor, helping individuals and families navigate life's challenges with faith as their anchor. His commitment to fostering a welcoming and inclusive community has made our church a place where everyone feels valued and connected.


Reverend Sarah Davis
Reverend Sarah Davis serves as our Associate Pastor, bringing a wealth of knowledge and a fresh perspective to our congregation. She holds a Bachelor's degree in Theology and has a heart for youth ministry and community outreach. Reverend Sarah is known for her dynamic sermons and her ability to connect with people of all ages. She leads our youth group with enthusiasm, guiding our younger members on their spiritual journey. Her commitment to social justice issues inspires our congregation to make a positive impact in our community and beyond.


Choir Director Emily Johnson
Emily Johnson is the creative force behind our church's vibrant music ministry. With a degree in Music Education and years of experience, she leads our choir and worship team with passion and precision. Emily's love for music is contagious, and her dedication to worship creates a spiritually uplifting experience for our congregation. She also coordinates special musical events and outreach programs that bring joy and healing to our community. Her leadership helps us express our faith through the beauty of song, connecting us with the divine in a meaningful way.


Administrative Team
Behind the scenes, our dedicated administrative team ensures the smooth operation of our church. Led by our Office Manager, Lisa Martinez, this group of individuals handles the day-to-day tasks that keep our church running efficiently. From managing schedules and coordinating events to handling financial matters and supporting communication within our congregation, they are the backbone of our organization. Their commitment to excellence and attention to detail enables our pastoral staff and volunteers to focus on our spiritual mission. Feel free to reach out to them for any administrative inquiries, and they will be more than happy to assist you in any way they can.
